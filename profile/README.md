### What is MaiGirma?

MaiGirma is a multi-asset yield aggregator built on Stacks that lets Bitcoin holders put their capital to work.

Deposit BTC, sBTC, or STX into isolated vaults and earn optimized, auto-compounded yield across Stacks DeFi protocols — without managing complex positions.

### How It Works

```
Deposit Asset  ──►  MaiGirma Vault  ──►  Yield Strategies  ──►  Auto-Compound
  (BTC/sBTC/STX)       (mgBTC/mgsBTC/mgSTX)   (Zest, Bitflow, PoX)    (Keeper Bot)
```

1. **Deposit** — Send BTC, sBTC, or STX to the corresponding vault
2. **Earn** — Vault routes your capital across the best yield sources on Stacks
3. **Compound** — Rewards are harvested and reinvested automatically
4. **Withdraw** — Pull your assets plus accumulated yield at any time
